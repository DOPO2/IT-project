About the University

MAHE is a private research university located in Manipal, Karnataka, India. It offers undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields, and is known for its commitment to excellence in education, research, and innovation. MAHE prepares students for the global workforce and has collaborations with institutions worldwide.

About MIT

MIT Manipal is a private engineering college located in Manipal, Karnataka, India. The college offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as computer science, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and more. It's renowned for its commitment to providing quality engineering education and conducting groundbreaking research.

About the Department

The Computer Science Department at MIT Manipal is dedicated to providing quality education in computer science and related fields. The department offers undergraduate and graduate programs in computer science, and has a strong focus on cutting-edge research, innovation, and entrepreneurship. It has a distinguished faculty who are experts in their respective fields, and the department collaborates with various academic and industrial partners to provide students with practical, hands-on experience and exposure to the latest technologies.
